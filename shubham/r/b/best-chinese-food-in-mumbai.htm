Types Of Chines Chicken: :


    Shalimar Chicken Chilli



    In Shalimar chicken chilli we make Cubes of boneless chicken, cooked with capsicums, green chillies in soya sauce. A Shalimar specialty. In this recipe, the boneless chicken is cooked within the soya sauce until they get that particular desired structure and aroma.


    Chicken Hot Black Garlic



    In chicken hot black garlic first, the bones of all the chicken are removed it is then fired which has some specific spices layering until it gets to its desired texture, then it's cooked in garlic sauce followed by chilli sauce and then tomato sauce.


    Chicken Tsinghai



    In chicken Tsinghai, the chicken is first diced into pieces and then it's cooked in very spicy red sauce.

    Lamb in Hot Bean Sauce



    In this particular dish, the Cubes of boneless lamb lightly coated and fried. Cooked with spring onion, ginger, chopped green chillies, black bean sauce, and a dash of soya sauce.

Chinese starters

Types Of Chinese chicken


Shalimar chicken Hungama


In Shalimar chicken Hungama the bones from the chicken pieces are first removed and the chicken is further sliced into tender small cubes, which are coated and fired respectively. In this dish, we will be providing you with a combination of two flavoured dishes that is pepper salt and Tsinghai.


Chicken 65


Chicken 65 is people's very own favourite and special when it comes to starters in chicken 65 Boneless chicken cooked with curd and curry leaves in very spicy red chili sauce.


Chicken 380 Dry


Same as that of chicken 65 in chicken 380 dry boneless chicken is sliced, coated and fried. Cooked with chilli tomato and paste tomato sauce, peanut sauce, and black bean sauce red in colour, sweet-sour in taste.


Types Of Chinese Mutton


Shalimar Mutton Hungama


In Shalimar Mutton Hungama boneless pieces of mutton are coated and fried. A combination of two flavored dishes Pepper Salt and Tsinghai.


Lamb in Hot Bean Sauce Dry
