Shalimar offers six types Shalimar Mughlai Foods :


Chicken Biryani

An all-time favourite of all foodies. Our traditional chicken biryani is made with long-grained basmati rice with aromatic herbs, saffron, hung curd, spices. Layering is done with marinated chicken. Has garam masala and is garnished with the barista. Our traditional chicken dum biryani is having savoured chicken, rice, and aromatic spices are steamed together.

Mutton Biryani

Our traditional mutton biryani is made with a pack of flavours, caramelized onions, saffron, fresh mint, and aromatic Indian spices. This biryani mutton is slow cooked which becomes tender and soft and then layered with half cooked basmati rice. All this is steamed together which makes it an authentic mutton dum biryani. It is the most sought-after platter Mughlai dish on our menu that is garnished with the barista and served.

Biryani Rice

An aromatic non-spicy mixed basmati rice that is cooked with Indian spices. It also includes a pinch of saffron and nuts that takes this dish to a whole new level. Our biryani rice has a balanced flavour of whole spices and is garnished with a barista or fried onions.

Fish Biryani

We make a traditional fish dum biryani that has long-grained basmati rice, whole Indian spices, aromatic herbs along with special biryani masala and curd. This perfect combination of fish with aromatic spices and condiments makes it a lip-smacking delicious.
