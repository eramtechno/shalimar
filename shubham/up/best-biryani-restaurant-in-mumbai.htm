Types Of Biryani :


    Chicken Biryani



    In the chicken biryani, the platter of traditional chicken dum biryani is created and then it is mixed mildly with our special garam masala which is made by our special chef's and further, in the end, it’s garnished with a barista.


    Mutton Biryani



    The second most loved biryani in Indian is also served at our restaurant which is cooked by creating the platter of traditional mutton dum biryani which is further mixed with our few special garam masalas and in the end, it's garnished with a barista.


    Chicken Tikka Biryani




    Other Indians favourite biryani because it comes with boneless and marinated pieces of chicken tikka cooked with Indian spices, curd and topped with rice finished with fried onion and cashew. Its heavy demand is what makes it unique in our Shalimar restaurant.


    Chicken Khapsa Rice



    Chicken Khapsa rice is the Chicken tandoori fried with Indian gravy and served with egg fried rice in traditional Indian style. It’s a combination of both tandoori chicken eggs with some great aromatic rice at the same time.


    Mutton tikka biryani



    In mutton tikka biryani the boneless and marinated pieces of mutton tikka cooked with Indian spices, curd, and topped with rice finished with fried onion and cashew.
