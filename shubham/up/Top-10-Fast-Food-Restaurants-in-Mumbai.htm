Top 10 Fast Food Restaurants in Mumbai.



Mumbai is a food paradise because of its rich and colourful history in a variety of foods. For a very long time, this wonderful city has been a constant place of both traditional and rich delicacies. It is also quite famous for some popular street food like Vada pav, cutting chai, breakfast items like bun-Maska, missal pav, etc. there are hundreds of people that come from very far places in search of money, businesses, jobs, fame, etc. all of them need some top fast food eating joints or restaurants that are quick, good in taste and suffice their hunger in few minutes.

Here we give you the ultimate list of exploring the top 10 fast-food restaurants in Mumbai.

1. Shalimar Fast Food Restaurant

A classic fast-food restaurant where one can divulge in all types of fast food meals. It has one of the best professional chefs that specializes in a wide variety of fast food items like sandwiches, rolls, burgers, Lebanese cuisines, and chatpata chaats. Shalimar Restaurant is regarded as one of the best fast-food restaurants in Mumbai. Shalimar restaurant located at bhendi bazaar, Mumbai..It starts from 11 am to 3 am-midnight.

2. Subway

Subway is an American fast-food chain of restaurants that have established their presence in Mumbai. A big brand that is worldwide famous for its sandwiches, salads, sides, and snacks. If you are looking to indulge in some quick, rich, and healthy sandwiches that subway should be on your mind. they are specialists in making one of the best salads and sandwiched around the world.

3. Shahi Roll centre

Shahi roll centre in Mumbai serves delicious rolls at minimal rates. It’s a small place but they serve the most amazing rolls and kababs. Popular dishes are Chicken Egg roll, paneer roll, and double egg roll.

4. Kailash Parbat

Kailash Parbat is an iconic name when it comes to fine dining in the best vegetarian cuisines. They make tasty fast food meals which are really great to taste and devour. For the past 7 decades, they have been delighting customers who have always cherished eating at this food heaven.

5.King's shawarma

king-sized shawarma having the most authentic taste from the middle east. This unique fast food was started for the sole reason to give shawarma lovers a taste of a lifetime. The chicken used in this shawarma and leaves you wanting more. They have a lot of variety of shawarma like chicken cheese shawarma, chicken olive shawarma, chicken pineapple shawarma, and more.

6. Faasos

It is quite famous for its wraps and rolls. Started in 2004 and since then it has become a loyal brand that has delighted every fast food lover. It provides fast food items like wraps, meals, rolls, biryani, and desserts. All its items are delivered online are lip-smacking delicious to taste.

7. Mughlai kitchen

This kitchen serves excellent fast-food meals like shawarma, pizza, melting sandwiches, etc. it provides takeaway and online delivery service. You can have a great fast food experience by eating delicious fast food meals at affordable rates.

8. Fried chicken Cuisine

Awesome pieces of fresh, tender fried chicken meals that will satiate your hunger joyfully. The chicken is marinated with spiced and seasoned flavours and then fried crisp.

9.McDonald’s

An all-time favourite for all of us. There hasn’t been any person I guess who wouldn’t have come to McDonald's. The happy meals with add-ons are always delightful to taste and the taste is legendary. For so many years still, we all love to relish these happy meals that consist of chicken burgers, French fries, and cold drinks.

10. KFC

Like all other fast-food restaurants KFC is also a great place to eat fast food meals. They are quite famous for their fried chicken pieces that have a distinctive taste full of goodness and mouth-watering. They provide fast food in dining at their fast food joints as well as online delivery at your home.


Fast food is a part of our daily lives; they are always delightful to munch on at any time of the day. These top 10 fast-food restaurants in Mumbai will surely keep you wanting for more if you are willing to taste them!
