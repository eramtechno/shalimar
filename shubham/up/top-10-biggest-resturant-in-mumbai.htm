Top 10 Biggest Restaurants in Mumbai



Mumbai city has a lot to offer in exploring places like historical monuments, beaches, film city, iconic cinema halls, etc. in all of these it is quite popular but in food, it is a divine place where one can eat all types of meals. An amazing nightlife, great street food around every nook and corner of this city is always there for you to experience it. And not to mention the fact that Mumbai street food or foods in restaurants are exciting and mouth-watering. You can get everything from simple Maharashtrian food, spicy chaats, to Mughlai and Chinese cuisines you will find it all in this city.

We are going to share with you the top biggest 10 restaurants in Mumbai, where you can have delicious food at affordable rates.

1. Shalimar Restaurant

Shalimar Restaurant is the top 10 restaurants in Mumbai. Shalimar Restaurant is the top 10 restaurants in Mumbai. Shalimar restaurant name stands above all that has been mentioned before. A rich legacy of serving exotic cuisines at this restaurant that has been there for 5 decades. Situated in one of the most sought-after areas in bhindi Bazar, Mumbai it has been the only choice for all the food lovers who come to visit this marketplace. Amazing comfort, warm ambience, and professional staff will keep you smiling always. It serves North Indian, Mughlai, Chinese, falooda. Vegetarian delicacies etc. not to forget its daily specials like soups, kababs, Nalli nihari are quite popular. A crowded place where everyone feels a taste of home. It has won many awards and accolades. Rates are also affordable that gives you the ultimate joy in dining.

2. Copper Chimney

The copper chimney is one of the best casual dining restaurants in Mumbai. It serves food in north Indian and Mughlai delicacies. All the meals are rich and delicious that always gives you a reason to try and eat. They also serve lavish buffets for big parties and celebrations. Located in a top-notch place in Worli but all the rates on their menu list are quite economical.

3. Arab Restaurant

It is located in sewri Mumbai. This restaurant serves delicious food in both vegetarian and non-vegetarian cuisines. Also, it is quite famous for its own style of making north Indian and Mughlai meals. All of their food has affordable rates where anyone can relish the food in all its glory.

4. Delhi Zaika

Delhi zaika is a restaurant that serves north Indian and Mughlai recipes. It is also famous for its exotic dishes of north Indian styles of kebabs and curries. they also serve Chinese cuisines. All the rates are affordable.

5. Bungalow 9

Bungalow 9 is actually a bungalow themed restaurant situated in the queen of suburbs Bandra, Mumbai. The place is luxurious and has an amazing ambience. The restaurant is decorated with all wooden-clad furnishing. It is usually crowded with people coming to enjoy various types of meals. It also serves an outdoor area that is fun for your friends and family get-together.

6. Persian Darbar

Persian Darbar is a multi-cuisine restaurant that has been serving rich and delicious Mughlai dishes over the last 4 decades. They have made themselves a symbol of trust, good taste, and providing a royal dining experience. With a distinctive taste in all their Mughlai cuisines, they have named as the “Biryani King of Mumbai.

7. Pani puri At Elco Market

This is one of the most famous snack places in Mumbai. It is situated in Bandra and is always crowded with food lovers who only come to this place to have Pani puri. It is regarded as one of the best in the city because it is tasty and crunchy. It is usually served with spicy and tangy water.

8. Chaats At Juhu Beach

if you are looking for some pastime then visiting Juhu beach and gorging favourite chaats should be your only choice. However, you always find snacks and chaats on any beach but the best place is this that serves tasty and popular chaats like pani puri, Sev puri, Dahi vada and ragada pattice.

9. Paramount hotel

the paramount hotel is situated in the busy streets of Mahim junction, Mumbai. This place serves awesome meals for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. It is actually a very typical old-school restaurant that opens from 6 am to 1 am.

10. Jaffer Bhai’s Delhi Darbar

one of the most prominent restaurant chains in Mumbai, which is 6 decades old. Delhi Darbar has a rich heritage of its own when it comes to serving tasteful meals. They always have a unique method of food preparations that are mixed with original recipes that are almost centuries old. Although they have some expensive delicacies overall the rates are reasonable.


Feeling hungry! Looking for eating and gorging snacks or food at these top 10 biggest restaurants in Mumbai, well then, we have got you covered. Take a joyful eating experience in these top eating places and we are sure you won’t be disappointed!
