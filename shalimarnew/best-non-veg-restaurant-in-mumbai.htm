Types Of Choices of Kebabs :


    Chicken Tandoori



    In chicken tandoori, our delicious halal chicken is marinated in curd and special grounded masala which is further barbecued on a charcoal sigri. The main thing about this dish is that it is barbecued on a sigri which mixes up all the spices with the halal chicken properly.


    Khazana kebab



    A Colorful platter of assorted kebab, Murg Mumtaz, Sholay, Reshmi, Pahadi kebab, Muslim Chicken Malaysia, Murg Kohinoor. Marinated will all the curd barbecued on a charcoal sigri. The khazana kebab remain on top when people desperately want to eat some meat but due to some reason it’s not available but here at the Shalimar restaurant we won’t disappoint you the kebab will be at your table and your opinion regarding it that “this is by far the best non-veg restaurant in Mumbai I ever visited”


    Reshmi kebab



    As the name itself tells everything about the kebab dish it’s all about the aroma and spices. So in Reshmi kebab selected chunks of halal chicken marinated in curd and cream with non-spicy and chosen herbs. Grilled on charcoal sigri coated with egg and cheese, till the aroma is released.


    Murg Mumtaz




    The Murg Mumtaz is the traditional dish on which Drumsticks of Muslim chicken stuffed with chicken kheema, marinated in curd and tandoori masala. Grilled on charcoal sigri till tender.


    Chaurangi kebab



    Hotel Shalimar Mumbai brings you its best type of kebab that is the chaurangi kebab which a combination of four types of kebab (Sholay kebab, pudina kabab, reshmi kebab, and methi kebab). This platter of four kebabs brings you all the types of spices and its heavy aroma which is more than enough for your endless cravings for kebabs.

Raan Tandoori


Raan Tandoori


Raan Tandoori is the dish which is not only everybody’s favorites but only on the top priority list when it comes to non-veg food. Here at Shalimar restaurant in Mumbai we provide you the best of our own authentic Raan Tandoori, along with tandoori raan masala, raan pepper salt and raan biryani because of course biryani is everybody’s non-veg category.

Types Of Raan Tandoori


Raan Tandoori


In Raan Tandoori we succulent leg of mutton marinated in curd and specially prepared red tandoori masala. Cooked on charcoal sigri till tender, releasing aroma. Served on a big platter along with the green salad. A delicacy of Shalimar.


Tandoori Raan Masala


In Tandoori raan masala, we succulent leg of mutton marinated in curd and specially prepared red tandoori masala. Cooked on charcoal sigri till tender. Poured onion based masala gravy over tandoori raan.


Raan Pepper Salt


In Raan pepper salt Mutton leg chopped in small pieces coated and fried. Accompanied by diced spring onion cornflour, french bean, and carrot. Cooked with chopped onions, garlic, ginger, green chili with a pinch of pepper salt.


Raan Biryani


In Raan biryani we succulent leg of mutton marinated in curd and specially prepared red tandoori masala. Cooked on charcoal sigri till tender. Served on a big platter, layered on the bed of masala rice partly covered with biryani rice. Decorated with slices of egg, tomato and sprinkled on top with brista and fried cashew nuts.



Tsinghai Raan

Tsinghai Raan

In Tsinghai Raan pieces of mutton leg cooked with garlic, celery, chopped onion in spicy chilly sauce, garnished with spring onions.
